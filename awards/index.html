About the Mari Awards

The Mari Awards are an annual recognition programme celebrating the best and most responsible companies in the online casino industry. Unlike many industry awards that are decided by votes, paid entries, or purely commercial criteria, the Mari Awards are determined entirely by our independent editorial and research team based on objective performance data, player feedback, and regulatory track records. Winning a Mari Award is a genuine mark of excellence.

The awards cover a wide range of categories reflecting the full spectrum of quality in online gambling: from the best overall casino experience and the most innovative game provider to the most responsible gambling operator and the best customer support team. Our assessment draws on the data we collect year-round through casino reviews, complaint handling, regulatory monitoring, and player surveys.

Award Categories

The 2026 Mari Awards span the following categories, with additional special recognition categories announced closer to the ceremony:

  • Casino of the Year — overall excellence across all evaluation criteria
  • Best New Casino — launched within the past 12 months
  • Best Game Provider — innovation, quality, and game library breadth
  • Best Live Casino Experience — quality of live dealer product and game selection
  • Best Mobile Casino — quality of the mobile playing experience
  • Best Customer Support — response times, resolution rates, and helpfulness
  • Most Responsible Casino — commitment to player protection and responsible gambling
  • Best Payment Experience — speed, variety, and reliability of deposits and withdrawals
  • Best Casino Bonus — fairness and value of bonus offerings
  • Industry Achievement Award — recognising outstanding contribution to the iGaming industry

How Winners Are Selected

All Mari Award winners are selected through a rigorous research-led process conducted entirely by our independent editorial team. No operator can purchase a nomination, pay for improved consideration, or influence the selection process through commercial arrangements. This independence is fundamental to the credibility of the awards and is non-negotiable.

The selection process begins with a longlist drawn from our database of reviewed casinos and operators. The longlist is based on quantitative performance data including review scores, complaint resolution rates, player satisfaction ratings, and regulatory compliance records. A shortlist is then developed through qualitative assessment by our senior reviewers, with final winners decided by a panel discussion and majority vote. Our methodology is documented and available for public review upon request.
