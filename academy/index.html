Mari Academy

Last updated: June 2026

What Is Mari Academy

Mari Academy is a free professional education programme designed to raise standards across the online casino industry. It provides structured, expert-led courses covering the most important topics in iGaming compliance, player protection, and responsible business operations. The Academy was created in response to a clear gap in accessible, high-quality training resources for people working in the online gambling sector.

All courses are available free of charge and are accessible online at any time. They are developed in collaboration with regulatory experts, responsible gambling specialists, and experienced iGaming practitioners. Mari Academy does not charge for access to its course materials or certificates, in keeping with our belief that raising industry standards should not be dependent on budget.

Who Is It For

Mari Academy is designed primarily for professionals working in the online casino industry, including customer support agents, compliance officers, marketing professionals, product managers, and senior executives. A well-trained team is the foundation of a responsible and legally compliant operation, and the Academy provides practical, applicable knowledge relevant to everyday roles in the industry.

The Academy is also accessible to anyone with a serious interest in understanding how the online gambling industry works from the inside — including researchers, regulators, journalists, and policy professionals. While the courses are designed with industry practitioners in mind, the content is clear and structured enough for informed outsiders to follow and benefit from.

Available Courses

The current Mari Academy curriculum includes the following core courses, with additional specialist modules being developed for release throughout 2026:

  • Responsible Gambling Fundamentals — understanding problem gambling, harm prevention strategies, and operator obligations to players at risk
  • Casino Compliance — overview of licensing requirements, regulatory frameworks, and the obligations of licensed operators in key jurisdictions
  • Player Protection — practical tools and policies for identifying vulnerable players and implementing effective protective measures
  • Anti-Money Laundering Basics — introduction to AML obligations in the gambling sector, know-your-customer requirements, and suspicious transaction reporting
  • Bonus Fairness and Marketing Standards — how to design promotions that comply with advertising standards and treat players fairly
  • Data Protection in iGaming — GDPR and data security obligations for online gambling operators

How to Enrol

Enrolment in Mari Academy courses is open to all. To access the course materials, create a free Mari account and navigate to the Academy section of the platform. All course materials are available immediately upon registration, with no waiting period or application process. You can complete courses at your own pace and return to materials at any time.

For operators who wish to enrol multiple team members simultaneously, we offer a streamlined group registration process. Please contact us via the Contact page for information on setting up a group enrolment for your organisation. Group enrolment is free of charge and includes additional reporting tools to track team members' progress.

Certification

Upon successful completion of each course, including passing the end-of-module assessment with a minimum passing score, participants receive a Mari Academy Certificate of Completion. Certificates are issued digitally and can be shared via a unique verification link, allowing employers, regulators, or other interested parties to verify the authenticity of the certificate independently.

While Mari Academy certificates are not regulatory qualifications, they demonstrate a genuine commitment to professional development and industry best practices. Several operators already reference Mari Academy completion in their job requirements and performance development frameworks. We are in ongoing discussions with regulatory bodies about formal recognition of the Academy programme.

Why It Matters

The online gambling industry has faced persistent criticism for failures in player protection, inadequate compliance practices, and a culture that has sometimes prioritised revenue over consumer welfare. Mari Academy exists to address these issues at the source: the knowledge and practices of the people who design, operate, and manage online casinos every day. Better-trained professionals make better decisions, and better decisions lead to safer, fairer products for players.

By making high-quality training freely available, Mari Academy removes the cost barrier that has historically meant that smaller operators and newer employees miss out on professional development. We believe that responsible gambling and compliance knowledge should be universally accessible within the industry, not reserved for large operators with training budgets. The long-term goal of the Academy is to contribute to a measurably higher standard of player protection and regulatory compliance across the entire iGaming sector.
